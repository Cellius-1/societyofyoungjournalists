About SYJ

Of the people, by the people, for the people.

The Society of Young Journalists is a student-run, independent journalism organization. Our mission is to provide accessible information and empower the next generation of journalists.

SYJ Times represents the voice of young journalists, helping students understand the importance of quality journalism and storytelling in today's world.
